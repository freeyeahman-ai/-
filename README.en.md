## Origin Story

Recently immersed in the study of *Zhouyi* (I Ching), I suffered a hand injury that made traditional divination with bamboo sticks impossible. With AI assistance, I built this simulator using the **Simplified Divination Method** (**Takashima Ekidan**) created by Master **Takashima Donsho** (高島吞象)—the most accessible entry point for beginners. After 40+ iterations of refinement, it's finally ready to share with the community.

## Features

- 🎋 **Slide-to-Split**: Simulate the authentic 49 yarrow stalks splitting process, supporting mouse/touch sliding
- 🕐 **True Solar Time**: Automatic calculation of true solar time and 12 Earthly Branches (requires location permission)
- 📚 **Complete Hexagram Library**: 64 hexagrams with detailed interpretations, citing *Takashima Ekidan in Vernacular Chinese*
- ⚡ **Moving Line Analysis**: Automatic calculation of original and transformed hexagrams, highlighting the moving line (51% importance)
- 🔒 **Privacy First**: Pure frontend implementation; your divination stays local only—no server, no tracking, no cloud storage
- 📱 **Responsive Design**: Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop

## Divination Taboos (Must Read)

This project strictly adheres to traditional I Ching taboos:

1. **One Question, One Divination**  
   The first divination receives the answer; repeated questioning of the same matter confuses the hexagrams.

2. **Maximum Three Times Daily**  
   The Way of Changes values tranquility; frequent divination leads to a scattered mind. One question per day is recommended; wait for signs to manifest before asking again.

3. **No Life or Death Questions**  
   Questions about lifespan and mortality are the greatest taboo—do not ask.

4. **Sincerity is Required**  
   Frivolous testing or disrespectful attitude receives no divine response.

## Usage

1. Download `index.html` and open it directly in your browser (no server needed)
2. Select gender (splitting sequence differs for male/female)
3. Meditate on your question and enter it in the input field
4. Slide to split the yarrow stalks; confirm when your mind is sincere and still
5. Repeat three times (Upper Trigram → Lower Trigram → Moving Line)
6. View the hexagram interpretation and moving line revelation

## Technical Details
